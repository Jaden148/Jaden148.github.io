THe Ultimate Anime Guide!

read time: 5 minutes

One Piece:
One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy sets sail to find the legendary treasure, the "One Piece," and become the Pirate King. Along the way, he forms a loyal crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, each with their own dreams and skills. Together, they face powerful enemies, the Marines, and other pirates, exploring strange lands while pursuing their personal goals

Demon Slayer:
Set in Taisho-era Japan, Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is slaughtered by demons. His sister Nezuko is turned into a demon, but retains some of her humanity. Determined to avenge his family and find a cure for Nezuko, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps. Alongside comrades like Zenitsu and Inosuke, Tanjiro faces powerful demons in intense battles, while uncovering the truth behind the origins of the demon race.

Naruto:
Naruto tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja ostracized by his village because he has a powerful Nine-Tails fox demon sealed within him. Despite his hardships, Naruto dreams of becoming the Hokage, the village's strongest ninja leader, to earn recognition and respect. Alongside his friends Sasuke, Sakura, and mentor Kakashi, Naruto battles formidable enemies, unravels dark secrets about his past, and strives to protect his village while overcoming his personal struggles.

Dragon Ball Z:
Dragon Ball Z continues the adventures of Goku, now an adult and a powerful Saiyan warrior. He and his friends defend Earth from a series of ever-stronger foes, from alien invaders like the Saiyan Prince Vegeta, to powerful beings like Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu. Along the way, Goku trains to become stronger, pushing the limits of his power, and teams up with allies, including his son Gohan and rival Vegeta, in action-packed battles to save the universe.

My Hero Academia:
In a world where nearly everyone has superpowers (called Quirks), My Hero Academia follows Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who dreams of becoming the greatest hero. After inheriting the powerful Quirk "One For All" from the legendary hero All Might, Izuku enrolls in U.A. High School to train alongside his classmates to become pro heroes. He faces villain attacks, personal challenges, and learns the responsibilities of heroism in a society where heroes are celebrated.

Author : Jaden Joseph
